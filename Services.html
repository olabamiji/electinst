Types of Electrical Wiring Systems

The electricity distribution board provides the electric supply up to the outside the consumer’s premises. The consumer has to take the connection from that point to the main switchboard at home.

From the main switchboard, various types of electrical loads such as fans, lights, room coolers(air condition), electric stoves, bathing water heaters, kitchen heat regulator(heat dispensers) and refrigerators are connected through the wires.There are different types of wirings used for connecting the loads to the mains which can be used for house electrical wiring as well as industrial electrical wiring. Two of these types are discussed below.

  1. Batten/Surface wiring
  2. Conduit wiring

    • Surface conduit wiring

    • Concealed conduit wiring

Batten/Surface wiring

In this, insulated wires are run through the straight teak wooden battens. The wooden battens are fixed on the ceilings or walls by plugs and screws. The cables are fitted onto the battens by using tinned brass link clips.

These clips are fixed to the battens with rust-resistant nails. This wiring installation is simple and cheap as compared to other electrical wiring systems also takes less time to install. These are mainly used for indoor installations.

In Nigeria, modern system of embraces surface system in which clips are fitted onto walls and ceilings directly. For this, no wooden bait is required.


Conduit wiring

In this type of wiring, insulated cables run around through pipe. Conduit pipes are either PVC pipes or metal pipes mostly produced with steel. This conduit wiring can be either surface conduit wiring or concealed conduit wiring.

Surface conduit wiring

If the conduit pipes are run on surface of the walls and ceilings, it is called a surface conduit wiring Surface conduit wiring is used in industries to connect the heavy motors. This system is relatively cost compare to concealed system because of the pipe produce by steel. PVC is cheaper than Steel.

Concealed Conduit wiring

If the conduits are run inside the surface of the walls and ceilings and are covered with plastering, it is called as concealed conduit wiring. Concealed wiring is the most popular and common method of wiring the residential buildings. The concealed conduit wiring is the safest method of wiring and also looks beautiful.
